What are focal flowers?

Focal flowers are what stand out most in an arrangement. You will be drawn to them due to either their size or their eye-catching features. This flower features one large bloom per stem.

What are secondary flowers?

Secondary flowers support the focal flowers in a bouquet without overpowering the main blooms. You can identify secondary flowers by choosing ones that are smaller or less bold than the focal flowers.
