What is a Filler?

Filler flowers fill out your arrangements and make them look full and complete. They feature clusters of smaller blooms. They're great for adding volume, texture, and variety to your designs

What is a Unique Filler?

A unique filler is an unconventional or eye-catching element that adds character and personality to a bouquet.

See the difference?

A normal filler is simple and fills empty spaces with little flowers or leaves.
A unique filler is more special-looking and adds fun shapes or colors to the bouquet.
