A full bouquet uses a blend of colors and textures to create a large arrangement. A focal flower captures attention at the center, supported by secondary flowers, while fillers and greens tie all the elements together.

Considerations

1. What is this person's favorite color(s)?

2. Do they have a favorite flower(s)?

3. Are they from a cultural background that might have taboos related to flowers?
