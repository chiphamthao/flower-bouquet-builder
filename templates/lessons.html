Lessons


Colors

Colors set the mood and tone of a bouquet, creating harmony or contrast among the flowers.


Focal Flower

The focal flower is the standout bloom that draws attention and anchors the entire bouquet.


Secondary Flower

Secondary flowers support the focal flower by adding fullness and complementing its color and shape.


Fillers

Fillers are small flowers or plants that fill in gaps, add volume, and blend all elements together.


Greens

Greens provide a natural backdrop that frames the bouquet and enhances its overall structure and flow.


Color Harmony

Color harmony brings balance and unity to your bouquet by combining shades that complement or contrast in pleasing ways.


Textures

Textures add depth and visual interest by combining smooth, rough, soft, or spiky elements.
